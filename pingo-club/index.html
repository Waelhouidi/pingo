About Us

"Pingo IT Club is a vibrant community that stands at the intersection of passion and innovation in the field of technology.Comprising four integral departments:

  • Development & programmation
  • Organization & sponsoring
  • Marketing & media

Pingo IT thrives on fostering an environment where members can grow, collaborate, and contribute to the ever-evolving tech landscape. The Development department focuses on hands-on learning and skill enhancement through workshops and collaborative projects. The Organization department ensures seamless execution of club operations and events. Sponsoring establishes crucial partnerships to support the club financially, while the Marketing & Media department enhances visibility through engaging content creation and social media strategies. Pingo IT has proudly achieved milestones, ranging from successful projects to collaborative events, and is poised for an exciting future with upcoming initiatives and expansion plans. Join Pingo IT in shaping the future of technology and experience a community that values both individual growth and collective success.

Portfolio

The portfolio for our club within the Organization of Graphic Design and Web Building (OGDWB) showcases a dynamic fusion of creativity and technical expertise. With a keen focus on graphic design, our portfolio highlights a diverse range of projects, including visually stunning branding materials, captivating print designs, and innovative digital artworks. Emphasizing our proficiency in web building, the portfolio also features a collection of responsive and user-friendly websites that demonstrate our commitment to delivering seamless online experiences. Through a harmonious blend of aesthetics and functionality, our portfolio serves as a testament to our club's dedication to pushing the boundaries of design and web development within the OGDWB community.
